About Us

Between the two of us, we have over 15 years of experience in the animal field. We’ve both worked with farm animals, exotic animals, reptiles, and of course, Dogs. As a prior veterinarian technician Jaylin has had experience delivering medication and vaccinations. We live and breathe animals, and can’t wait to help your family out anyway we can!

Services

Boarding/Daycare

Everything K9 is the full-service solution to your daycare and boarding needs. Relax knowing your pet is under the care of Charlie and Jaylin Fowler, professional dog trainers. Whether your dog prefers solitude or social time, we’ve got them covered.

Dog Boarding School
(3 Week Boarding & Training)

Unleash your dog’s potential with our 3-week board and train program in our home. We teach sit, down, come, heel, place and kennel—plus address behavioral issues like barking or leash pulling. Includes leash, place bed, e-collar, and long line. Lifetime support provided.

Lessons

If you’re not ready for the board & train, we also offer personal lessons. We cover basic commands and help you correct unwanted behaviors, using leash or E-collar methods. We’ll guide you every step of the way!
